From Wikipedia: Modiolus philippinarum is a species of "horse mussel", a marine bivalve mollusc in the family Mytilidae. This species is present in the Red Sea, Madagascar and Indo-Pacific, from eastern Africa, to eastern Indonesia; north to Japan and south to Queensland and Western Australia. It inhabits sublittoral muds, at depth of 0-40m.
